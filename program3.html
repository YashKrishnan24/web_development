Ladakh, the "Land of High Passes," is a region in Northern India defined by its craggy mountains, crystal clear lakes, and ancient Buddhist monasteries. It is a haven for bikers, trekkers, and those seeking silence.

Must Visit Spots

1

Pangong Tso Lake

The world's highest saltwater lake that changes colors from blue to green.

2

Nubra Valley

Ride the double-humped Bactrian camels in the high-altitude desert dunes.

3

Magnetic Hill

A gravity-defying hill where vehicles appear to roll uphill on their own.
