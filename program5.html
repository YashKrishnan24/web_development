The Core Concepts

UX (User Experience)

The science behind the design. It focuses on how a product feels and functions.

  • Goal: Solve user problems efficiently.
  • Key Tasks: User Research, Wireframing, Prototyping, Testing.
  • Analogy: Designing the blueprint and structure of a house.
vs

UI (User Interface)

The art of the design. It focuses on how a product looks and interacts.

  • Goal: Create beautiful, intuitive interactive surfaces.
  • Key Tasks: Typography, Color Theory, Icons, Layouts.
  • Analogy: The interior decoration, paint, and furniture.
